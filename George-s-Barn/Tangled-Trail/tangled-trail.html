Tangled Trail

A nature walk from the barn to the museum

Explore the Trail

En route along the Tangled Trail keep your eyes open for the flora and fauna on view. Pause at the Dipping Pond then continue to the museum.

Whilst enjoying George's Barn why not obtain a copy of the map and work your way up to the Railway Museum.
