Railway Museum

Step back in time to a 1930s railway station on the Elham Valley Line

Step Back in Time

It is just a short walk from the Countryside Centre to the Railway Museum, where for a modest charge, you can be transported back to a 1930s railway station as it would have been on the Elham Valley Line. You can wander round our station building and look at the historical local and railway memorabilia or watch the model train sweep along the Elham Valley from Canterbury to Folkestone. Car parking is at George's Barn.
