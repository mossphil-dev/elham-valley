Group Visits & Education

School trips, group outings and birthday parties

Group Visits

We welcome school and group visits and can offer an insight into the history of the Elham Valley Line. Subject to volunteer availability, we can supply you with a bespoke package.

What we can offer:

  • • Large car and coach park
  • • Food and drink
  • • Pond dipping
  • • Miniature train rides
  • • Visits to George's Barn and the Railway Museum

Birthday Parties

We can organize that special birthday party for your child and their friends. Please pay us a visit and ask for Emma to learn more.
