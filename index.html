Explore Our Attractions

From a 1930s railway station to a 290-year-old countryside barn

George's Barn

A 290-year-old barn housing a museum of countryside tools, crafts and household items.

Railway Museum

Step back in time to a 1930s railway station on the Elham Valley Line.

Tangled Trail

A nature walk linking the barn to the museum through woodland and past the dipping pond.

Crazy Golf

Fun for all the family on our outdoor crazy golf course set in the museum grounds.

Preserving History Since 1984

The Elham Valley Line Trust was formed in 1984 dedicated to preserving railway history, countryside crafts and to provide educational facilities. We are a registered charity run solely by volunteers.
