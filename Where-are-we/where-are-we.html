Where We Are

George's Barn is our main entrance with free parking

Getting Here

George's Barn and the Railway Museum are located in the village of Peene, near Newington, Folkestone, Kent.

Free parking is available at George's Barn. The Railway Museum is a short walk along the Tangled Trail from the barn.
