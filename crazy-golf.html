Crazy Golf

Fun for all the family in the museum grounds

Test Your Skills

Our outdoor crazy golf course is a firm favourite with families visiting the trust. Set in the grounds between George's Barn and the Railway Museum, the course features a variety of fun obstacles and challenges that will keep children and adults entertained.

With windmills, ramps and tricky bends, each hole offers a different challenge. It is the perfect way to enjoy the outdoors while the kids burn off some energy before or after exploring the museum and barn.

Something for Everyone

The crazy golf course is suitable for all ages and abilities. Young children can have a go with help from parents, while older kids and adults can compete for the best score. Clubs and balls are provided.

The course is available during normal opening hours and is included as part of your visit. After your round, why not grab a cup of tea from our tea room and enjoy the views across the Kent countryside.
